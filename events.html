About Us

By SoloDevs, For SoloDevs

Welcome to SoloDevelopment, a community built by solo developers for solo developers! We provide a space where indie devs can connect, share experiences, and support each other on our solo game development journeys.

We regularly host game jams and encourage developers of all skill levels to participate. Whether you're looking to collaborate, get feedback, or just learn from others, this is the place for you!
